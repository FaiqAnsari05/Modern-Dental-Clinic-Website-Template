Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our dental services

Do you accept dental insurance?

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Our team will work with you to maximize your benefits and handle all the paperwork. We also offer flexible payment options for treatments not covered by insurance.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

If you're experiencing a dental emergency, please call us immediately at (123) 456-7890. We reserve time in our schedule for emergency cases and will do our best to see you as soon as possible, often on the same day.

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend visiting the dentist every six months for a routine check-up and cleaning. However, some patients with specific dental conditions may need to visit more frequently. Your dentist will recommend a schedule tailored to your oral health needs.

Do you offer sedation dentistry?

Yes, we offer several sedation options to help you feel comfortable during your dental treatment. These include nitrous oxide (laughing gas), oral sedation, and IV sedation for more complex procedures. We'll discuss the best option for you during your consultation.

What COVID-19 safety measures do you have in place?

We follow all CDC and ADA guidelines for infection control. Our safety measures include enhanced sterilization protocols, air purification systems, mandatory mask-wearing, temperature checks, and social distancing in waiting areas. We also offer teledentistry consultations when appropriate.
